Professional Endeavors

Ramaglia is an avid writer and has published multiple articles for online platforms such as The Odyssey, and is currently pursuing an internship to write for cognifit.com. In the summer of 2019, she worked as a teacher for students with special needs at the Elaine Clark Center. In the summers of 2017 and 2018, she worked as a camp counselor at the URJ Camp Coleman. She is currently on the social programming committee of Hillel at UGA's executive board and works on marketing events to Jewish students. Ramaglia is also a research assistant in Dr. Leonard Martin's evolutionary psychology laboratory and attended a study abroad trip in March 2019 focused around social work and womens' health. In addition to her work experience, she has received the accolade of being on the University of Georgia's Dean's List.
